# ShadowCast 動画プロンプトガイド（詳細版）

> 作成：2026-03-24
> 対象ツール：Veo 3.1（テキスト → 動画、ゼロショット）

---

## 広告の核心メッセージ

> 「AI英会話アプリにぼったくられている。
> でも生成AIは今や誰でも持っている。
> 正しいプロンプトさえあれば、英会話練習はほぼ無料でできる。
> それを作るのが ShadowCast。」

---

## 登場人物の設定（全動画共通）

キャラクターの一貫性を保つため、毎回同じ文言で書くこと。

**主人公（男性）**
```
a Japanese man in his late 20s, slim build, short straight black hair,
light skin, wearing a faded grey crewneck sweatshirt and dark chino pants.
He has a quiet, earnest expression — the kind of person who genuinely
wants to improve but feels a bit lost.
```

**英語の話し方（重要）**
```
When he speaks English, he has a noticeable Japanese accent:
he pauses briefly between phrases as if translating in his head,
slightly elongates vowels in the Japanese way (e.g., "very" sounds closer
to "bery"), and drops articles occasionally. His tone is earnest and
serious — not embarrassed, just clearly a learner working hard.
This is NOT fluent English. It is the English of someone who has been
studying for two or three years and is still building confidence.
```

## 動画① 「価格への怒り → 気づき」（25秒）

**コンセプト**：AI英会話アプリが高すぎることへの怒りと、「自分はもう持っている」という気づき

```
SCENE DESCRIPTION:
A 25-second cinematic advertisement. No narration. Minimal dialogue.
The entire emotional arc is conveyed through the character's face,
environment, and sound design.

--- OPENING SHOT (0:00–0:06) ---
Medium close-up, slightly low angle, handheld camera with subtle
organic movement. A Japanese man in his late 20s — slim build, short
straight black hair, light skin, faded grey crewneck sweatshirt — is
sitting on a couch in a small, lived-in Japanese apartment at night.
Warm practical lamp light from the left, cool blue glow from his
smartphone screen on his face. He is scrolling through the pricing
page of an English learning app. His brow furrows slightly.
Cut to: extreme close-up of the smartphone screen — a pricing table
visible with monthly fees in the range of ¥3,000–¥10,000 per month,
Japanese text, clean app interface aesthetic. Hold for 2 seconds.
Cut back to: his face. He exhales through his nose. Not dramatic —
just a quiet, tired frustration. The expression of someone who has
seen this before.

--- MIDDLE SHOT (0:06–0:16) ---
He lowers the phone. Stares at the middle distance for a moment.
Then something shifts — a slight tilt of his head. He glances at
his phone again, but this time he opens a different app: the Gemini
or ChatGPT interface. Slow zoom-in on his face as recognition crosses
it — the quiet "wait a minute" moment.
Camera: locked-off medium shot, very slight push-in over 8 seconds,
building intimacy. His expression moves from tired frustration → pause
→ quiet realization.
Lighting: the cool phone glow intensifies slightly as he looks at the
AI app screen.

--- FINAL SHOT (0:16–0:25) ---
Wide shot of the same apartment. He opens a browser on his phone,
navigates to a simple web interface (shadowcast.app aesthetic —
dark, minimal), taps a button, and a prompt is generated on screen.
He copies it, pastes it into the Gemini interface. A small, calm
smile. Not triumphant — just satisfied.
Hold on his face for 2 seconds. Slow fade to dark navy background.
White text fades in: 「生成AIは、もう持っている。」
Then below it: 「shadowcast.app」 in smaller electric blue text.

AUDIO:
0:00–0:06: Quiet apartment ambience — distant city sounds through
a window, refrigerator hum. Subtle sound of finger swiping on
a glass screen.
0:06–0:16: Near silence. Just room tone. The quiet gets louder as
his realization builds — this silence is intentional and should
feel contemplative.
0:16–0:25: A single, clean piano note. Then two more, slow and
minimal. Fade to near-silence under the final text card.
No dialogue. No voiceover. No subtitles. No subtitles!

VISUAL STYLE:
Cinematic 4K. Color grade: desaturated cool tones in the first half
(frustration), warming slightly in the second half (realization).
Film grain overlay — subtle, 35mm aesthetic. Shallow depth of field
throughout. No jump cuts — only slow, deliberate transitions.
```

## 動画② 「Before/After：練習シーン」（20秒）

**コンセプト**：同じ人物が、練習前後でどう変わったかを正直に見せる

```
SCENE DESCRIPTION:
A 20-second split-narrative cinematic short. Two scenes, same
character, same desk, different time of day and emotional register.

--- BEFORE SCENE (0:00–0:10) ---
Medium shot, handheld camera with slightly unstable framing — the
instability mirrors the character's internal uncertainty. A Japanese
man in his late 20s — slim, short black hair, faded grey crewneck
— sits at a small wooden desk in his apartment at 11pm. A laptop
is open in front of him playing an English audio clip. He listens,
then attempts to repeat the sentence aloud.

His English is clearly non-native:
He says something like: "The... the meeting will be... held on...
uh... Friday." He pauses mid-sentence, replays the audio on the
laptop, tries again: "The meeting will be held on... Friday."
Better, but the rhythm is stiff, the "held" is slightly over-
pronounced, and "Friday" has a faint Japanese intonation on the
second syllable. He does not look embarrassed — he looks focused
and slightly tired. He pauses, makes a small correction gesture
with his hand (touching his mouth briefly), replays again.

Lighting: harsh cool-white desk lamp, heavy shadows, blue laptop
glow. The room feels isolating.
Camera: handheld medium shot slowly drifting toward close-up on
his face, tracking the emotional weight.

--- TRANSITION (0:10–0:12) ---
Slow dissolve — the harsh desk lamp light transitions into warm
morning sunlight. The audio of his hesitant English fades under
the transition.

--- AFTER SCENE (0:12–0:20) ---
Same desk, same man, same grey sweatshirt — but now it is 8am
and the room is filled with soft, warm morning light from a
window behind him. He wears one white wireless earbud. He is
practicing the same type of sentence, but this time there is a
noticeable improvement: the pauses are shorter, the rhythm is
smoother, and he self-corrects mid-sentence naturally rather than
stopping to replay.

He says something like: "The meeting will be held on Friday
morning, and we need to... prepare the report." His accent is
still clearly Japanese — "meeting" has a slight elongation,
"report" ends with a Japanese-style flat tone — but the overall
flow is more confident and rhythmic. He doesn't touch his mouth.
He glances at the screen and gives a single slow nod.

This is NOT fluency. This is the English of someone who has been
practicing earnestly for a few months and is starting to feel it.

Lighting: warm golden hour light, soft shadows, far more open
and airy than the before scene.
Camera: locked-off medium shot, very slow push-in over 6 seconds.
Shallow depth of field — desk and laptop gently blurred.

AUDIO:
0:00–0:10: Late night room ambience — faint hum of city outside.
Laptop audio briefly audible (a native English speaker's voice,
very faint). His own hesitant English clearly audible.
0:10–0:12: Ambient sound fades during dissolve.
0:12–0:20: Morning birds faintly in background. His more
confident (but still accented) English clearly audible.
Underneath: a very quiet, warm acoustic guitar tone — single
notes, not a melody, just texture.
No voiceover. No subtitles. No subtitles!

VISUAL STYLE:
Cinematic 4K. Before: desaturated, cool-toned, contrasty.
After: slightly warmer, softer contrast, more open shadows.
Same subtle 35mm film grain throughout. Rack focus from
background to character at the start of the After scene.
```

## 動画③ 「使い方30秒デモ」（30秒）

**コンセプト**：「3ステップで始められる」を画面と人物で見せる

```
SCENE DESCRIPTION:
A 30-second cinematic product demonstration video. Clean, modern
aesthetic. The goal is to show that the entire workflow — generate
prompt, paste into AI, start practicing — takes under one minute
and costs nothing extra.

--- SHOT 1 (0:00–0:07): The Problem Setup ---
Extreme close-up of a smartphone screen showing a competitor
app's paywall screen — blurred/anonymized, showing only a
price range "¥3,000〜/月" in Japanese. A finger hesitates
over the "subscribe" button. Does not tap. Screen goes dark.

Camera: static, macro lens aesthetic. Very shallow depth of field.
Lighting: cool white backlit screen glow in a dark background.
Audio: a single UI "blocked" sound — like a lock clicking shut.
Silence after.

--- SHOT 2 (0:07–0:17): Opening ShadowCast ---
Cut to: over-the-shoulder shot of the same Japanese man (late 20s,
short black hair, grey crewneck) at his desk, opening a browser
on his laptop. He navigates to a simple dark-themed web app
(shadowcast.app aesthetic). The UI is visible: a settings panel
with simple options. He adjusts two toggles — level, topic — and
clicks a large button labeled "プロンプトを生成".

Camera: over-the-shoulder, medium shot, then slow push-in toward
the laptop screen. The UI should be legible but not need to be
read in detail — the simplicity of the interface is the message.
Lighting: warm desk lamp, soft practical light. Evening apartment.
Audio: quiet keyboard sounds, a subtle confirmation chime when
the button is pressed.

--- SHOT 3 (0:17–0:25): Paste Into Gemini ---
Close-up of his hands copying the generated text from the browser
and pasting it into a Gemini interface on the same laptop. The
Gemini chat window is visible — a long block of text appears in
the input field. He presses Enter.

Camera: close-up on hands and keyboard, then rack focus up to
his face as he leans back slightly and puts in an earbud.
Lighting: same warm lamp, with the screen glow shifting from the
shadowcast.app dark UI to the lighter Gemini interface.
Audio: paste keyboard shortcut sound (Cmd+V, Enter), then the
faint chime of Gemini processing. A moment of anticipation silence.

--- SHOT 4 (0:25–0:30): Practice Begins ---
Medium shot. He leans forward, listening to Gemini's voice
through his earbud, then begins repeating a sentence quietly
but audibly — English with a clear Japanese accent, earnest
and focused. A very small, almost imperceptible nod as he
gets through the sentence.

Slow fade to dark navy. White text appears: 「あとは、話すだけ。」
Below: 「shadowcast.app — 無料で始める」 in electric blue.

AUDIO:
0:25–0:30: His voice clearly audible — non-native English, the
word "the" pronounced "ze", slight stiffness in phrasing, but
confident in effort. Underneath: a warm minimal electronic
music bed fades in softly from 0:20, building gently to the
end card.
No voiceover narration. No subtitles. No subtitles!

VISUAL STYLE:
Cinematic 4K. Consistent color grade throughout — warm, slightly
desaturated, film-grain overlay. Clean, modern, no gimmicky
transitions. Hard cuts between shots for a crisp pacing.
```

## Veo 3.1 プロンプト使用時の注意

| 注意点 | 対処 |
|--------|------|
| 勝手に字幕が入る | 必ず末尾に `No subtitles. No subtitles!` を書く |
| 音声が英語ナレーションになる | `No voiceover. No voiceover narration.` を明記 |
| 英語が流暢すぎる | `clearly non-native`, `noticeable Japanese accent`, `pauses as if translating` を毎回書く |
| キャラクターが途中で変わる | 外見・服装の描写を毎プロンプトに完全コピーして入れる |
| 観客の笑い声が入る | `Audio:` セクションに ambient sound を必ず指定する |
| テキストが崩れる | 映像内のテキストはなるべく少なく・短く、または最終カードのみに限定する |

---

## 制作順序（推奨）

```
1. 動画② Before/After（コンセプトの核）を最初に生成・確認
        ↓
2. 動画① 価格への怒り→気づき（感情的フック）
        ↓
3. 動画③ 使い方デモ（最後に追加）
        ↓
4. CapCut で①②をつなぎ、日本語テロップ・BGMを整える
```
